Every frontier model promises to reshape the world. ORYZO-1 promises to keep your table dry. It does exactly one thing, and it does it with absolute conviction.

02 / CAPABILITIES

Three behaviours. Faithfully reproduced.

001

Table Protection

A dense cork substrate absorbs condensation and arrests heat transfer before it reaches the surface beneath. Zero-shot. No fine-tuning required.

002

Perfect Circularity

94mm of uncompromising rotational symmetry. Every approach vector is the correct approach vector. Rotationally invariant by design.

003

Thermal Moderation

Passive, parameter-free temperature handling across the everyday beverage distribution — from iced to scalding — with no observable drift.

04 / OPEN RESEARCH

Released openly. Because the world deserves coasters.

We are releasing ORYZO-1 in a clean, dependency-free OBJ format under a permissive license. Run it locally. Render it. Simulate it. Drop a virtual mug on it and watch nothing spill.

  • ↳ Single-mesh OBJ + MTL, no rigging required
  • ↳ Reproducible WoodenBench harness included
  • ↳ Quantized variant for low-poly edge devices
  • ↳ Model card with honest, unhelpful limitations
